The Problem

Desk work can hurt you quietly, every single day.

Lance is built for the worker directly, not generic policy checklists. We focus on the daily risks that build up over time: posture stress, eye fatigue, missed breaks, dehydration, and too much sitting.

The Solution

Lance coaches you in real time while you work.

Instead of one-size-fits-all training, Lance provides personalized in-the-moment coaching directly through your computer, helping you avoid strain before it turns into pain.

As your day unfolds, Lance watches for early risk patterns and gives simple, supportive nudges that are specific to you.

  • Posture and eye-health checks throughout your work session
  • Hydration, break, and movement reminders at the right moment
  • Clear, friendly coaching that helps you build safer habits over time

Beyond live nudges, Lance gives trend insights so you can spot recurring risks early and improve your daily routine proactively.

Personal Coaching Features

Built for computer-based workers who want to stay healthy, focused, and pain-free.

Posture Coach

Detects early posture drift and gives instant cues to reduce neck, shoulder, and back strain.

Eye Wellness Checks

Tracks screen exposure patterns and reminds you to rest your eyes before fatigue builds up.

Hydration Nudges

Provides simple prompts that help you maintain healthier hydration throughout your shift.

Break + Move Prompts

Spots long sedentary periods and prompts short movement breaks to protect your body.
